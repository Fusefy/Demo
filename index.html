Onboarding Agent

Our Onboarding Agent is a RAG-powered AI assistant that combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation with LLMs to deliver accurate, real-time responses to employee onboarding queries. It intelligently fetches policy, compliance, and access data from internal sources to eliminate repetitive HR tasks. This scalable solution ensures fast, consistent onboarding while reducing operational overhead.
ROI highlights: Lower HR costs, faster onboarding, improved compliance, higher satisfaction, and no delays during peak hiring.

Interview Agent

Our Interview Agent leverages Agentic AI to automate the end-to-end hiring process — from screening role-based resumes to scheduling interviews via calendar analysis. It autonomously coordinates with candidates and interviewers, sends invites, and collects feedback through a seamless chat interface. The system finalizes selections and dispatches offer letters automatically, streamlining recruitment with zero manual effort.
ROI highlights: Faster screening, auto-scheduling, reduced manual effort, timely feedback, improved hiring outcomes.
